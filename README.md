in-mist

> Send what you're unsure of, gently into the mist.

---

 What is this?

**An app that does not "solve" your indecision.**

Write down what you're unsure of, send it into the mist, and three lights will appear:

- choose
- stay in the mist
- don't choose

Whichever you choose, you are affirmed.

At the end, a single light remains.

---

 Who is this for?

- Those who have grown a little tired from always seeking the right answer
- Those who would rather quiet the heart than reach a conclusion
- Those who treasure stillness
- Those who love the world of Kenshi Yonezu's *Machigaisagashi*

There is no spectacle. No entertainment.
**Five minutes, in silence, returning to yourself.**

---

 Try it

🌫️ **[in-mist.vercel.app](https://in-mist.vercel.app)**

Just open it in your browser. No installation.


 Behind the work

This app was inspired by Kenshi Yonezu's song *Machigaisagashi (The Spot the Difference)*.

> "Inside all these mistakes, like a single right answer..."

I have lived seeking the right answer. This app was born from a wish — to tell myself, and others like me: *it's okay to be unsure. You, who have wandered, were enough.*

Tech
